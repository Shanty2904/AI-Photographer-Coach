What it does

A coach that sees
what your eye misses.

01

Live Composition Analysis

Every frame of your camera feed is analyzed against rule of thirds, subject placement, leading lines, and horizon leveling. You get instant feedback rather than reviewing bad shots later.

02

0–10 Quality Score

A live score and letter grade — A through F — shows you exactly how strong your current framing is. Watch it climb as you adjust your shot, and capture when you hit your peak.

03

Tilt & Rotation Guidance

An arc dial shows your exact tilt angle down to the decimal — and tells you which way to rotate and by how many degrees. No more crooked horizons.

04

One Focused Tip at a Time

Instead of overwhelming you with everything at once, the coach gives one actionable tip with a 7-second window to act. Once you've applied it, the next suggestion appears.
